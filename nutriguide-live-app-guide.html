📋 5 Tabs — What Each Does

💬 AI Chat Tab 1
  • Ask anything about nutrition
  • Quick chips: Breakfast, Weight Loss, Protein, Diabetic
  • Typing indicator while AI thinks
  • Markdown-formatted responses
  • Press Enter to send, Shift+Enter for new line
📊 Dashboard Tab 2
  • BMI calculator with animated gauge
  • TDEE using Harris-Benedict formula
  • 3 calorie targets: loss / maintain / gain
  • Macro split bar chart (carbs/protein/fat)
  • 5 activity levels to choose from
🗓️ Meal Plan Tab 3
  • AI-generated 1 to 7 day plans
  • Choose cuisine: Indian / South / North / Gujarati…
  • Set calorie target with a slider
  • Health goals: diabetes, PCOS, pregnancy…
  • Copy plan to clipboard
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Tab 4
  • Add up to 10 family members
  • Per-member: age, diet, conditions, allergies, goal
  • Profiles are injected into every AI prompt
  • One-click "Family Meal Plan" button
  • Edit or delete any member
🔍 Food Lookup Tab 5
  • Search any food item
  • Calories, protein, carbs, fat, fibre
  • Health benefits & tips
  • Quick chips: Idli, Dal, Roti, Paneer, Brown Rice, Milk, Banana, Chicken

💡 Pro Tips

👨‍👩‍👧
Add family members first — then every AI chat response will be tailored to your whole family's allergies, conditions, and goals automatically.
📊
Use the Dashboard first — calculate your TDEE, then use that calorie number when generating a meal plan for accurate targets.
🌙
Dark mode is the moon icon top-right — your preference is saved in the browser.
💧
Water tracker in the Chat sidebar — tap the 💧 glasses to log each glass. App celebrates when you hit 8!
📋
Copy meal plans with the Copy button — paste into WhatsApp, Notes, or print for the kitchen.
⚙️
Customize the agent anytime in agent_config.py — change tone, add diet types, edit safety rules. Save the file; next request uses the new instructions instantly.
